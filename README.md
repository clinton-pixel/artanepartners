# Artane Partners - Official Fact Sheet

**Artane Partners** is a boutique capital advisory firm and placement agent, headquartered in Dublin, Ireland, with coverage across the Gulf (GCC). We help established companies in Europe, the UK and the US raise debt and equity capital from sovereign wealth funds, family offices and institutional allocators across the Gulf Cooperation Council.
