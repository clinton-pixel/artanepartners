What the firm is, and is not

Artane Partners is a capital advisory firm and placement agent. It is not a bank, merchant bank, deposit-taker, investor or fund manager. It never takes custody of investor or client money, never asks anyone for account passwords, and never asks candidates to pay fees. Capital flows directly between the investor and the company.

Trading since 2024; the legal entity Artane Partners Limited was incorporated in Ireland in 2025. The firm has no Dubai office - its only office is the Dublin headquarters; Gulf cities are where its investor coverage is based.

Two addresses in the public record, one company

The firm's office is 13 Adelaide Road, Dublin 2 - also the legal address on its GLEIF LEI record. The Irish CRO register separately records the statutory registered office at Unit 2, 2 Bridge Street, Athlone, Co. Westmeath. A registered office is a company's official address for statutory correspondence under Irish company law and is not necessarily its place of business. Both records describe the same company, CRO 795432.

Entities with similar names that are NOT Artane Partners
